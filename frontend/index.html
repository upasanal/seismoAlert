Our Mission

At SeismoAlert, our mission is to ensure the safety of our community by providing real-time earthquake alerts and instant communication channels for those in affected areas. Inspired by the frequent earthquakes in Nepal and my Nepalese background, this web app aims to make a real difference in people's lives by offering timely alerts and resources for those in need.

Real-Time Alerts

Receive immediate notifications of seismic activity directly to your device, allowing you to stay informed when it matters most.

Live Communication

Connect with others in affected areas using our live chat feature, and receive support during emergencies.

Safe Zone Locator

Find nearby shelters and safe zones quickly with our integrated locator feature to guide you to safety.

Why This Matters

Earthquakes are an unfortunate reality in Nepal, putting family and friends in danger. SeismoAlert is here to bridge the gap between safety and timely information, giving people the power to act quickly in times of emergency. We aim to provide peace of mind by ensuring that everyone has access to the tools they need to stay safe.
